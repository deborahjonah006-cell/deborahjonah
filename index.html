Professional Biography

I am a mission-driven leader and the 2025 Ambassador for a Day. My career vision is centered on improving global healthcare access through the power of Data Analytics. I chose this path because I believe that healthy communities are the engine of productivity, and every individual deserves the opportunity to live a full life.

What makes me unique is my ability to combine technical analytical skills with deep empathy. While I enjoy interpreting trends and generating insights to solve real-world problems, I lead with emotional intelligence. I am not afraid to show my humanity—whether it is being moved by a touching film or empathizing with a community's challenges—this sensitivity ensures that my data-driven decisions always remain people-focused.
